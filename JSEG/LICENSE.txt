JSEG image segmentation program, by Yining Deng and B.S. Manjunath, both UCSB.
Converted into an OpenCV compatible library and made thread-safe by Chris Marcellino, UCSF, 2011.

For information on the algorithm, please visit:
http://vision.ece.ucsb.edu/segmentation/jseg/
